Sarah Harris

Independent Archaeologist

I am a passionate and dedicated independent archaeologist with over 7 years of experience in the field. My work focuses on uncovering the mysteries of the past and preserving our cultural heritage through careful excavation, research, and analysis.

Work Experience

  • Principal Archaeologist, Turner Archaeological Services (2016 - Present)

    Led archaeological excavations, supervised teams, and collaborated with local museums and universities.

  • Researcher and Field Archaeologist, Arup (2019 - 2023)

    Participated in expeditions, assisted in excavation and documentation, and contributed to research on Maya, Roman, and Neolithic cultures.

  • Excavation Site: Ancient City of Pompeii, Italy (2010-2013)

    Led a team, discovered well-preserved frescoes and mosaics, and contributed to understanding the eruption of Mount Vesuvius.

Research Interests

  • Prehistoric civilizations and development
  • Cultural exchange and trade in ancient times
  • Historical landscape reconstruction through geospatial analysis
  • Ethical considerations in archaeology and heritage preservation
